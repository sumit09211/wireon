Gyaansaar

Our product “GYAANSAAR” is designed for the modern-day changing landscape of the educational challenges and opportunities. The world is changing at a faster speed and the things which were considered to aberration yesterday, are the norms for the today and hence it becomes critical for all of us to embrace this changing environment. The young students are the future of the country and hence it becomes imperative to mould their mind in such a manner that they get acquaint with the changing processes and discover their latent talent. Gyaansaar is an end to end digital solution which would help to make the Institute smart. Gyaansaar has three core elements: Data Intelligence, Wisdom Management and Business Intelligence which helps in transforming Data into Information so that knowledge can be built and wisdom can be acquired. The offering is driven by the Artificial Intelligence, Natural language processing and Machine learning technology. The product helps in keeping track of the entire activities happening inside the institution. Statistics and Analytics are the core feature of the portal and all the values and solutions (in the form of graphs) are derived from the theories of the modern day proven statistical theories.
